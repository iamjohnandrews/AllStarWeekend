Frequently asked questions:

Q:How long will my flyer be featured?
A: Your flyer will be featured for 7 days in the designated area, according to the plan you purchased. Your plan is activated and flyers posted within 24 hours of your purchase.
Q:For the Franchise Player plan, what main slider on the Home page will my flyer be in?
A: Any slider you want. You can choose what background you want (Blue, Grey, Violet). If you cannot decide, the Franchise Player plan is for only one slider, but you can purchase another Franchise Player plan and have your flyer on multiple sliders.
Q:Why should my team promote through Weekend VIP?
